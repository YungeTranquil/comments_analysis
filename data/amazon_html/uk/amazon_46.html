Customers also asked

Can I still use my Amazon Mastercard?
Amazon Mastercards can no longer be used. NewDay has been in contact with all customers to share details on next steps.
What will happen to my reward points?
If you had an Amazon Platinum Mastercard you could earn Amazon Reward Points up until 11th November (unless you chose to close your account before this date). Your points balance will have been automatically converted into a gift card and was loaded to your Amazon account in December 2022. All accounts were credited to the penny, for example, 1024 pts = 10.24 gift card credit.
When can I apply for the new Amazon rewards credit card?
Our brand-new rewards card will be launching soon. Well be in contact with more details nearer the time, and to notify customers when they can apply.

Great Card For Amazon Customers - Recommended!
I read some negative reviews which I must be honest put me off applying for a while. However, the enticement of free money was too powerful and so I went for it and applied!So glad I did. The bad reviews I read have not reflected my own experience of this card, which has been a totally positive, easy and enjoyable experience.The application process was smooth and no different to any other credit card application (and this process is linked to your credit score/history so its understandably robust but nothing to worry about).I was expecting the automatic credit thing but that didnt happen for me, but I was informed that I would have to wait a few days for the decision as to whether I would be accepted or not (this is usually the case with me, but might be different for you).The confirmation email to say that I had been accepted for the card came after a few days and with it the confirmation that the 10 Amazon voucher would be added to my Amazon account (after the qualifying period).About a week after applying my card arrived and again, everything to date has run smoothly. I downloaded the app, effortlessly set up my direct debit and started to use my card.The 10 voucher was added to my account and I spent it in the same way as any other Amazon e-voucher and my monthly direct debits have run smoothly. I have had no problems using this card or managing the card account.So, I have absolutely NO complaints and would thoroughly recommend this card.Finally, a word about the reward points. As with all such cards, this card is not so great if you spend outside of Amazon. Yes, you can use it anywhere but the return on points is significantly lower (read the card details carefully to see what I mean). However, your spending on Amazon is quite well rewarded (at the time of writing this review) and so I tend to only use my card for my Amazon spending and I use an alternative reward card for spending with other outlets.In summary, its a great card, its free and you get rewarded. Whats not to like? Theres even the usual balance transfer offers, which might also come in handy .
