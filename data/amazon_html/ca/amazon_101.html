Maybe you're new to the platform, or perhaps you're just not tech-savvy enough to navigate the process smoothly.

Whatever the reason, the frustration of not being able to access your favorite shows and movies can be overwhelming.

Well, FEAR NOT! This short user guide will walk you through every step of the process, from navigating to the sign-in screen to entering the code and accessing all the amazing content that Amazon Prime Video has to offer.

With easy-to-follow instructions and helpful screenshots, this guide will make entering your Amazon Prime Video sign-in code a breeze. You'll learn how to navigate to the sign-in screen on your TV, how to retrieve your sign-in code from your Amazon account, and how to enter the code using your TV remote.
