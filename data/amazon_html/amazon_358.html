Finished the free trial and I'm keeping it. By using both Hulu and Philo with my new Firestick I get all the channels that I watched on cable -- except, unfortunately, The Weather Channel -- but for a lot less $ a month (even with Hulu's recent price increase). So, be sure to check first, if local channels are important to you, too. The only thing I've found so far that I don't care for is that Hulu's on-screen guide for live streaming tv only shows what's on "now" and what's "up next" rather than showing a schedule for the whole day/evening/week. Makes it hard to plan ahead for what to watch or record and especially for what to record if you're not going to be home.
